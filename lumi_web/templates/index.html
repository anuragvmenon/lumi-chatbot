About Us

Lumi is a mental health chatbot built to provide a safe, pampering, and supportive space for individuals to express themselves and seek guidance. Our mission is to make mental health support accessible and cozy for everyone, everywhere.

  • Anonymous and confidential conversations
  • Evidence-based techniques and resources
  • Available 24/7 to chat whenever you need

We understand that seeking help for mental health can be challenging. Lumi is designed to be a friendly, retro tamagotchi-like companion on your journey to well-being. Whether you're feeling down, anxious, or just need someone to pamper you, Lumi is here to listen and support you.
